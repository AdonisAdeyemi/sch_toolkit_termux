DASHBOARD AI IDEAS

I'd leave the control where it is (Current Term Information) and display only the status on the dashboard.

Here's why:

The dashboard should answer "What's the current state?"

The settings page should answer "How do I change it?"


That separation keeps the dashboard clean and prevents accidental changes.


---

Dashboard Statistics (future)

I would display something like this:

Card	Example

👨‍🎓 Students	524
🏫 Classes	18
📚 Subjects	24
📅 Current Period	2025/2026 • 2nd Term
🔒 Results Status	Teacher Lock
⚠ Awaiting Enrollment	12
📝 Incomplete Results	7


Notice that Results Status is simply informative.

Example:

Results Status

🟢 Open

or

Results Status

🟡 Teacher Lock

or

Results Status

🔴 Permanent Lock

If an admin wants to change it, clicking the card (or a small "Manage" link) can take them to Current Term Information.


---

Controller (future)

Eventually your dashboard controller might prepare something like:

$totalStudents
$totalClasses
$totalSubjects

$currentSession
$currentTerm

$resultsLockStatus

$studentsAwaitingEnrollment

$studentsWithIncompleteResults

Then pass them to the view:

return $this->view(
    'dashboard/index',
    compact(
        'totalStudents',
        'totalClasses',
        'totalSubjects',
        'currentSession',
        'currentTerm',
        'resultsLockStatus',
        'studentsAwaitingEnrollment',
        'studentsWithIncompleteResults'
    )
);


---

Awaiting Enrollment

This one is straightforward.

Count students that exist in students but have no record in report_student_departments for the current session.


---

Incomplete Results

This is one of the most valuable statistics.

It could count students who:

are enrolled,

should have results,

but are missing one or more subject scores.


Class teachers will immediately know work remains.
